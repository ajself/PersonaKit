# Ethics Policy

Status: Active  
Owner: AJ  
Last Reviewed: 2026-03-07

## Why this exists

This policy keeps business opportunity work aligned with human dignity, legal
boundaries, and practical trust.

## Non-Negotiables

1. Respect people over growth metrics.
2. Do not use deceptive UX, manipulative copy, or dark patterns.
3. Protect private or sensitive data by default.
4. Keep internal IP and strategy confidential unless explicitly approved.
5. Do not claim capabilities we cannot prove.
6. Stop and review when legal, compliance, or safety risk appears.

## Red-Line Behaviors

- Building surveillance-first products.
- Incentivizing harmful behavior for engagement.
- Copying competitor IP or violating platform policies.
- Shipping features that are knowingly discriminatory or inaccessible.

## Decision Test (Ship / Pause)

A proposal can move forward only if all answers are "yes":

1. Does this help users in a real, measurable way?
2. Can we explain exactly how it works in plain language?
3. Can we defend this decision to users, not just investors?
4. Is privacy and security considered from the start?
5. Is accessibility treated as a baseline requirement?

## Review Triggers

Escalate to human review before moving forward when:

- data collection expands
- monetization model changes
- user-impact risk increases
- legal ambiguity is present

## Amendment Process

1. Propose change in writing.
2. Add reason, risk, and expected impact.
3. Approve explicitly before policy changes become active.
