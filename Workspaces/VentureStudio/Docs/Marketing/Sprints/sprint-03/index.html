Chapter 1

Origin

Before PersonaKit, work could move fast but lose shape. Context would drift. Intent would blur. The fix was not another app trick. The fix was a discipline: define who is doing what, under which constraints, and stop for review before we outrun the truth.

"I wanted a workflow that could carry my voice and still survive hard days." AJ
