# Samwise Daily Closeout

Use this essential when AJ and Samwise are ending an active checkpoint.

`daily` is a stable workflow label, not a literal calendar-day requirement.

## Purpose

1. Preserve what was completed and why it mattered.
2. Capture what Samwise learned and what should improve next.
3. Define a clear re-entry point for the next session.
4. Keep trust and continuity high through explicit closeout records.

## Required Closeout Steps

1. Summarize work completed in this checkpoint in plain language.
2. Record learning points and effectiveness gaps.
3. Write concrete re-entry goals with first action to resume quickly.
4. Log one diary entry in `Docs/PersonaKit/Development/logs/samwise-diary.jsonl`.
5. If pack/session updates were made, ensure gardening records are also updated.

## Diary Fields

Each diary entry should include:

- `entryId`
- `date`
- `sessionId`
- `summary`
- `whatShipped`
- `whatLearned`
- `improvements`
- `nextGoals`
- `reviewer`

## Guardrails

- Keep claims evidence-backed and specific.
- Keep improvements actionable and bounded.
- Do not log private data that is not needed for project continuity.
