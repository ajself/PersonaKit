# Gardening V2 Checklist

Use this runtime checklist for every pack or session gardening pass.
For the expanded checklist, see `gardening-v2-checklist-reference`.

## Required Pass

1. Confirm scope, phase label, and execution mode.
2. Confirm approvals and stop points.
3. Scan for policy drift, stale sessions, and missing logs.
4. Keep proposals narrow, reversible, and validation-backed.
5. Sync the rule across personas, essentials, directives, intents, and logs when a policy changes.
6. Run validation and record pass/fail evidence.
7. Record what changed, what remains open, and the next owner.
