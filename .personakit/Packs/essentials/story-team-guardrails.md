# Story Team Guardrails

Use this essential for cross-role narrative website collaboration.

## Guardrails

1. One active stage at a time.
2. Every stage produces a concrete artifact.
3. High-severity review findings block progression.
4. Prefer plain language and emotional clarity over jargon.
5. Keep internal IP boundaries explicit.

## Quality Bar

- Story is understandable by a non-technical reader.
- Technical claims are evidence-backed.
- Website is accessible and responsive.
- Team handoffs are deterministic and documented.
